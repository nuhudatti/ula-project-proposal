⚖️ BMI Calculator

🧠 How This Project Was Built

This BMI Calculator was designed as a beginner-friendly yet modern practice app. It’s part of EvoLearn Africa’s mission to turn Python and Web skills into tangible micro-projects you can deploy, demo, and teach from Day 1.

🌱 Where the Idea Came From

  • Goal: Make health education digital, visual, and personal.
  • Approach: Use simple math (BMI formula) to teach forms, logic, inputs, and DOM manipulation.
  • Design: Make it visually impressive with Tailwind, yet readable on mobile or desktop.

🧩 Code Breakdown (In Plain Language)

<form>: This collects your height and weight. It waits until you hit "Calculate".

parseFloat(): Converts your input (string) into a number JavaScript can calculate.

weight / (height * height): The real math behind BMI – it’s that simple.

toFixed(2): Rounds your BMI to just two digits. Clean UI is good UX.

if...else: This part checks what BMI range you’re in and shows a message.

textContent + classList.remove('hidden'): Makes the result appear once everything works.
